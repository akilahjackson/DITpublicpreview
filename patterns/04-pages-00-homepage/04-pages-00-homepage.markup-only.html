Latest News

  • How to Make Your Teaching More Accessible Workshop

    ATI accessible learning instructional design specialist Sue Johnston will co-present at a September 15 Teaching and Learning Transformation Center diversity and inclusion workshop, 'How to make your teaching more accessible: Disabilities & universal design.' Registration is requested.

  • School Closed but Offices Still Open the Day Before Thanksgiving

    Recently, the Office of the Provost announced a change to the academic calendar. Beginning Fall 2016, the Wednesday before Thanksgiving will no longer be a class day; however, it will remain a normal business day. The semester overall retains the same number of class days, for both Monday/Wednesday/Friday and Tuesday/Thursday schedules, with the last day of classes on Monday, December 12. Please see the 2016-2017 Academic Calendar at http://www.provost.umd.edu/calendar/16.cfm for more details.

  • Free Training : Conquering Google Calendar

    A new course has been added to the free Google Learning Series offered by DIT -- Conquering the Calendar. If you are interested in learning how to more effectively use Google Calendar, including the basics as well as more advanced topics like managing shared calendars, adding video conferences, and understanding permissions issues, visit go.umd.edu/google-training to see the available dates and sign up for a session.
